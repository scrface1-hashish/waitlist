What Moxie Does / Doesn’t

  • Helps you pause, reflect, and reframe.
  • Suggests small, kind next steps for calmer talk.
  • Spots patterns over time to guide better choices.
  • Doesn’t diagnose, treat, or provide therapy.
  • Doesn’t offer legal, medical, or crisis advice.
  • Doesn’t replace professional help when needed.

Privacy & Safeguards

  • Your words stay yours — stored securely with modern standards.
  • No public sharing; we don’t sell personal data.
  • Moxie learns overall patterns, not personal stories.
